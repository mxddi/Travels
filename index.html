HOME TRAVELTHROUGH THE LENSABOUT




SEVENTEEN

January 4th, 2019



Today, my friends and I drove down to Bellevue Square to celebrate my birthday yesterday. I had a great time, and a wonderful start to the new year! We strolled around the mall for a few hours, shopping and trying on perfumes at Bath and Body Works (of course). After getting pretty hungry from all the looking around, we decided to eat at Panda Express. I obviously ordered chow mein, because it's the best item on the menu (proven by me). Later, we decided it was time to head back, especially since my grandparents are in town. We got in the car, tired and ready to go home...only to realize the car didn't start. After around twenty minutes of trying to get it back running again, we managed to have some success. At home, my stepmom baked me a vegan birthday cake. I love cake, and vegan cake is hard to come by so it was a pleasant surprise! All in all I'm feeling really positive heading into the new year. Looking forward, I have a lot of great things to anticipate, including traveling back to Isla Mujeres in the spring, an exchange program in Europe during the summer, spending time with friends and taking endless pictures of my cats! My resolutions include keeping up a positive outlook on life and taking time to do the things I love.

Au revoir! ;D
